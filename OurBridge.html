Welcome to the "bay" part of Sheepshead Bay!



This is where this little Brooklyn neighborhood got its name from!

Well, sorta...

Contrary to popular belief, and what I used to think when I first moved here, Sheepshead Bay does not refer to the shape of this bayside neighborhood.

Sheepshead actually refers to the name of a fish that used to be incredible common in local waters, especially in the early 19th century. So much so that fishing for the Sheepshead became a popular way farmers made money when waiting for the harvest. Since the late 1800s, however, these fish have mysteriously disappeared and are now seldom found this far north of the Atlantic. Learn more
