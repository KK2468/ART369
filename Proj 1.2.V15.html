Sitting across from me is Chie, dressed in her forest green corduroy pants, facing her laptop in deep concentration. You could tell she was thinking, even when she reached for the teapot to pour tea into our ceramics mugs on the table, she remained pensive...deep in thought...as though she was HERE but not quite. The many sounds (oh so many sounds at once) of the start of the weekend all permeating through the door, didn't seem to bother her. She was lost in thought, and so appeared oblivious to the endless chatter, the loud throwback songs the loud music, yet faint to our ears, the suite opposite us played.
She is a musician...a violinist,to be exact. But that night, the keyboard became her instrument. Together her typing sounds and the gurgling water boiler harmonized, not caring that inside we are quiet, while our neighbors across the hall are probably having the Friday Night of their lives; with Dancing Queen as the anthem of their youth.
