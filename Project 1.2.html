Variation 13

corduroy pants

Chie is wearing her new culottes, her corduroy pants. She is sitting in the lounge chair in the common room, sipping the Oolong tea from her ceramic mug as she works on her laptop. Her fingers dance across the keyboard; the typing sounds harmonizing with the sounds of the gurgling water boiler. A few moments after this chorus has ended, she pours the hot water into a small teapot. Taking another break from thinking before going back to the click clack of the computer.


Dancing Queen

It's Friday night, which means the start of the weekend. That's always fun and something to look forward to, I guess... There's bound to be something happening, and this time it's our neighbors. There were so many sounds at once, so many sounds all just PERMEATING through the doors. Compared to them, our suite was quite the opposite; a different ambiance of sorts because we lacked the loud music,, the chatter, and the beat of drums pounding in our ears (not that it wasn't through the doors already), drowning out the senses. And while they are dancing to the beat of throwback songs like Abba's Dancing Queen...


...inside, we are quiet...

and curled up with a good book. I am curled up with a good book I am sitting on the warm futon, surrounded by the remnants of Vale's things. Things that she left behind because she couldn't take it all with her back to Singapore. So, at that moment, I was surrounded by the patterns of her former belongings, and the pillows, the blue, yellow, (amarillo) pillows that we bought for the futon. I look around and see Chie's Medjool Dates, sitting on the table, half full, and in between a small, silver spoon and my smartphone on the wooden table, which stood on a wooden floor. Across from me is Chie's fridge, which, for now, belongs to the suite as a whole. And on the shelves of our common room, there are a plethora ofbooks, a collection belonging to each of us, and in this way, belonging to all of us...

I look up at the wall. It is bare except for the flu shot schedule and the cleaning schedule Yulan taped up. We called it minimalist decoration. It was cold, but it looked warm, but that was a lie...all a trick--a tromp l'oeil--created by the warm lighting of the lamp. So, it was warm but not really because of the open window, letting the crisp air, the nighttime, and the cold and wind inside. Now, I realize that my feet are COLD.

textingin dark mode on my phone...I see my two bracelets and the sleeves of my pajamas...drifting off to sleep... and memories flash by...i am home away from home...then...

Lost in Thought

There are too manywords in my head, and yet I feel empty. It makes me pensive, and I just want to sleep it off, so I can be away from the HERE & the now...so I can be content, and so II dont have to miss...miss...i miss... water...I want to sleep but I am not sleepy...I'm actually quite AWAKE... I just want to be free...away from the words in my head, so I can feel tranquil for once, without worry about communication, or about not getting phone calls or getting too many of them. I just want to go to SLEEP so that way I can finally be transcending time & space...
